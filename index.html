Welcome to the Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose Institute of Education!

We are a non-profit trust dedicated to improving the lives of people in our community through education, healthcare, and social welfare. We are named after the great freedom fighter Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose, who was a firm believer in the power of education to transform lives.

Our mission is to provide quality education to all, regardless of their background or circumstances. We offer a variety of educational programs, including preschool, medical college, nursing school, and various other higher education institutes. We also work to promote literacy and numeracy among adults.
In addition to education, we also work in the areas of healthcare, public awareness, and social welfare. We organize free health camps for the poor and marginalized people living in the most remote areas of Uttar Dinajpur district. We also conduct public awareness campaigns on issues such as menstrual health, sanitation, and gender equality. Along with that we also provide financial assistance and administrative support to people in need.
We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to live a life with dignity. Through education, healthcare, awareness, and social welfare, we are working to create a more egalitarian and equitable society.
